CPS643 Virtual Reality Project

Thievery VR Game


Game Description:

Thievery is a stealing stealth VR game.

You play as a thief that has broken into a mansion with the intent of stealing all its valuables. But watch out, guards stand between you and your precious loot. Sneak your way through the mansions various unique rooms and avoid getting caught.

The aim of the game is very simple:

GET LOOT GET OUT

This game is our project submission to both CPS643 and LevelUP Toronto
